Hadees 1: Niyyath ki Ahmiyath

Hazrat Umar bin Khattab (رضی اللہ تعالٰی عنہ) se riwayat hai, unhon ne kaha, Maine Allah عَزَّ وَجَلَّ ke Rasool صَلَّی اللہ تَعَالٰی عَلَیْہِ وَاٰلِہٖ وَسَلَّمَ ko farmatay hue suna: A'maal (ka sawab) niyat hi par hai, har shakhs ke liye wohi hai jo usne niyat ki. To jiski hijrat Allah aur Rasool عَزَّ وَجَلَّ وَ صَلَّی اللہ تَعَالٰی عَلَیْہِ وَاٰلِہٖ وَسَلَّمَ ki taraf ho, uski hijrat Allah aur Rasool عَزَّ وَجَلَّ وَ صَلَّی اللہ تَعَالٰی عَلَیْہِ وَاٰلِہٖ وَسَلَّمَ ki taraf hai, aur jiski hijrat dunya ki taraf ho, jise woh hasil kare ya kisi aurat ki taraf ho jisse woh nikah kare, to uski hijrat usi ki taraf hai jisne hijrat ki.. (Sahih Bhukari: 2529) Wazaahat:

Musanifeen-e-Hadith umooman apni kitab ki ibtida mein is Hadith ko la kar is baat ki taraf ishara karte hain keh tahsil-e-ilam se qabal niyat ki durustgi zaroori hai. (Makhooz az Ash'at al-Luma'at, Vol1, P35)

Is Hadith ka ma'na yeh hai keh a'amal ka sawab niyat par hai, baghair niyat kisi amal par sawab ka istehqaq (yaani haq) nahi. A'amal amal ki jam'a hai aur is ka atalaaq azaa, zabaan aur dil teenon ke af'aal par hota hai, aur yahan a'amal se murad a'amal-e-saleha (yaani neek a'amal) aur mubah afaal hain. Aur niyat lughawi tor par dil ke pakhtaa iraaday ko kehte hain aur shar'an ibadat ke iraaday ko niyat kehte hain. Ya darkein keh ibadat ki do qismain hain:

(1) Maqsoodah: Jaise namaz, roza keh in se maqsood hasil-e-sawab hai inhen agar baghair niyat ada kiya jaaye to yeh sahi nahi honge is liye keh in se maqsood sawab tha aur jab sawab mafqood ho gaya to is ki wajah se asal shay adaa nahi hoga.

(2) Ghair Maqsoodah: Woh jo doosri ibadaton ke liye zari'a hon jaise namaz ke liye chalna, wuzu, ghusl, waghera. In ibadaton-e-ghair maqsoodah ko agar koi niyat-e-ibadat ke saath karega to isse sawab milega aur agar bila niyyat karega to sawab nahi milega magar in ka zari'a ya wasila banna ab bhi durust hoga aur in se namaz sahi hojayegi. (Makhooz az Nazhat al-Qari Sharh Sahih al-Bukhari, J1, S226)

Ek amal mein jitni niyatein hongi utni nekiyon ka sawab milega, maslan muthaj qaribat daar ki madad karne mein agar niyat sirf Lillah (yaani Allah Azza wa Jal ke liye) dene ki ho to ek niyat ka sawab paayega aur agar silla-e-rahmi ki niyyat bhi karega to dohra sawab paayega. (Ash'at al-Luma'at, J1, S36)

Isi tarah masjid mein namaz ke liye janabi ek amal hai is mein bohat si niyatein ki jasakti hain, Imam-e-Ahl-e-Sunnat Maulana Shah Ahmad Raza Khan علیہ رحمة الرحمن نے Fatawa Rizwiyah Jild 5 Safha 673 mein is ke liye chalees niyatein bayan ki hain aur farmaya: Be shak jo ilm-e-niyat janta hai ek ek fail ko apne liye kayi kayi nekiyan kar sakta hai.

Balkay mubah kaamon mein bhi achi niyat karne se sawab milega, maslan khushboo lagane mein ittiba-e-sunnat, ta'zeem-e-masjid, farhat-e-dimagh aur apne Islami bhaaiyon se napasandida buudoor karne ki niyatein hoon to har niyat ka alag sawab hoga. (Ash'at al-Luma'at, J1, S37)

Madinah: Achi achi niyaton se mutalliq Rahnumai ke liye, Ameer-e-Ahl-e-Sunnat Damaat Barakatuhum Al-Aaliyah ka Sunnaton bhara bayan 'Niyat ka Phal' aur niyaton se mutalliq aap ke murtab kardah cards ya pamphlet Maktabat al-Madinah ki kisi bhi shaakh se haasil farmaiye.

Fikr-e-Madinah: Kya aap ne aaj kuch na kuch jaiz kaamon se pehle achi achi niyatein ki? Niyaton ko kam az kam do ko is ki tarhib dilayi.
